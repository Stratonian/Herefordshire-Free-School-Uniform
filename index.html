About

Hello and welcome to the Herefordshire Free School Uniform website.

On this website, you can view our current stock of our school uniform.
If you find what you are looking for, click on the Facebook icon to go to our Facebook page and then message us for collection details.

You can sort each row alphabetically by clicking on the headers.
You can search for a specific item by typing keywords in the searchbox in the top right.
There may be multiple pages of items that you can search through by clicking the "Next" and "Previous" buttons on the bottom right.
You can also select how many items you would like to display per page by clicking the dropdown menu on the top left.
